Chocolate Ice Cream

Description

Chocolate Ice Cream recipe features an ultra rich, thick, and creamy double chocolate custard-based ice cream with chocolate fudge swirled throughout. Every bite is a chocoholic's dream!

Ingredients

  • a cup of milk
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt
  • three lightly beaten egg yolks
  • Chopped semisweet chocolate
  • two cups of heavy cream
  • A teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring the milk, sugar, cocoa, and salt to a simmer in a saucepan.
  2. Whisk ½ of the milk mixture into the yolks, return to the pan, and stir until thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chopped chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate in a chilled bowl until cold.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients, then freeze in an ice cream maker.
